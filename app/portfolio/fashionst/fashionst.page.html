FASHIONST

A ramp for fashion passionates
  • FashionST is a WordPress website developed for all the fashion passionates.
  • FashionST is a fashion show event organized for the people who want to build their career in the fashion industry.
  • It provides a platform for all the participants to learn new and various aspects of modeling and grooming them towards aspects of fashion.
  • It's a One Page website that includes details about various opportunities for the participants. Any individual can register himself/herself for the event for the auditions.
