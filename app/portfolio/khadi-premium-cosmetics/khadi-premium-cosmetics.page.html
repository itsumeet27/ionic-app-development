KHADI PREMIUM COSMETICS

A premium range of natural cosmetic products
  • Khadi Premium Cosmetics is a brand introduced by Luka Enterprises
  • It is an E-Commerce website for the purchase of natural beauty cosmetic products.
  • The website includes the integration of the PayUMoney payment gateway for online purchase whereby the user gets an invoice for any purchase made online.
  • The website also includes a section where it displays the Instagram feed of the business that helps in engaging the audience and connecting socially.
  • Another version of Khadi Premium Cosmetics is developed using the boilerplate method as discussed in E-Commerce Website Development with the specified features.
